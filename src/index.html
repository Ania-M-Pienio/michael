Getting It Done Attitude, and Minimal Viable Products (MVPs)

I build software to solve problems and make someone's life better. I strive deliver solutions as early and small as possible to get feedback and build better. I enjoy working with stakeholders to balance fast delivery with developing quality solutions that are maintainable in the future.

A Security Perspective

I've helped clients secure their crowned jewels with automation, threat modeling, and compliance activities. I am super excited to put a security lens on software development to produce applications that protect the confidentiality, integrity and availability of its users.

Building High Quality Software

I am a strong beleiver in building software with as few defects as possible. I incorporate test-driven developement (TDD) when its appropriate because fixing defects early, or preventing them, saves resources and reduces risk of production issues.
